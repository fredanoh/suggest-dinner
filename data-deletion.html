Data Deletion Request

At Suggest Dinner, we value your privacy. Because our app is designed to be "local-first," we do not store your personal information on our servers.

How to delete your data immediately:
1. Open the app and go to Profile & Settings.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Reset Onboarding.
3. This will instantly wipe all local records (meals, pets, family names) from your device.

Manual Deletion via Android

You can also delete all data by following these steps on your device:

  • Go to device Settings > Apps > Suggest Dinner.
  • Tap Storage & Cache.
  • Tap Clear Storage or Clear Data.
